About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Computing and Information Technology, Great Bay University. Previously, I received my Ph.D. from the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. (Under the supervision of Prof. Jialin Zhang and Prof. Xiaoming Sun.) Before that, I received a B.Sc. from the Hua Loo-Keng class at Shandong University, majoring in applied mathematics. I am interested in several directions of theoretical computer science and machine learning, including bandit theory, submodular maximization, and mechanism design.
