About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences under the supervision of Prof. Jialin Zhang and Prof. Xiaoming Sun. Before that, I received a B.Sc. from the Hua Loo-Keng class at Shandong University, majoring in applied mathematics. I am interested in several directions of theoretical computer science and machine learning, including bandit theory, submodular maximization, and mechanism design.

My Erdős number is 3 (me -> Xiaoming Sun -> László Babai -> Paul Erdős).

I am actively looking for a job starting in the fall of 2025.
